Our Story

Born From a Burning Desire to be Different

At Sakred Fire, our passion for flavorful food stems from a lifelong love for diverse cuisines. Inspired by the selflessness of our grandmothers, who fed medical students and dedicated their lives to nursing, we aim to bring joy and happiness to others through our creations.

In 2022, fueled by a passion for cooking, we embarked on a journey to create a unique hot sauce. After experimenting with various peppers, we realized we wanted to retain the flavor of the spices without overwhelming heat.

Refined through extensive tastings by friends, family, and colleagues — this is a sauce made by the people, for the people. Made by hand in small batches from our Toronto kitchen.
