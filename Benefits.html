Our Process

Two Approaches, One Exceptional Sauce

Method One
Lacto-Fermented Hot Sauce

A traditional process that uses natural fermentation to develop complex flavours and powerful gut health benefits.

Improved Digestibility

Fermentation breaks down complex compounds, making food easier for the body to process and absorb.

Distinctive Flavours & Textures

Fermentation brings unique taste profiles and aromas that enrich the entire culinary experience.

Gut Health Benefits

Rich in natural probiotics that promote better digestion and a balanced, healthy gut microbiome.

Reduced Sodium Content

Natural fermentation minimizes the need for added salt while fully preserving — and enhancing — flavour.

Anti-Inflammatory Properties

Contains probiotics and bioactive compounds that may help reduce inflammation in the body.

Method Two
Pasteurized Hot Sauce

A carefully controlled heat process that ensures food safety, extended shelf life, and consistent quality in every bottle.

Extended Shelf Life

Pasteurization eliminates harmful bacteria and microorganisms, preserving the sauce safely without refrigeration.

Enhanced Consistency

Pasteurizing ensures a more consistent flavour and texture by stabilizing ingredients and preventing spoilage.

Retains Flavour Integrity

While it slightly alters raw flavour, pasteurization preserves the essential taste components of every sauce.

Increased Commercial Appeal

Pasteurization makes the sauce safer and more appealing to consumers who seek longer-lasting, trusted products.

Preserved Colour & Appearance

Heat treatment helps prevent colour changes in the sauce, keeping it visually vibrant and appetizing over time.

Why It Matters

The Hot Sauce That's Good For You

Most hot sauces are made for heat seekers and thrill chasers. Sakred Fire was made for everyone else — and specifically for people who love flavour but pay for it later with heartburn, acid reflux, or digestive discomfort.

Our unique formulation philosophy delivers a brief, satisfying sting that disappears in seconds, never travels past the tongue, and never lingers to cause discomfort. The heat exists to complement the flavour — not to punish you for enjoying your food.

Combined with ingredients like ginger, apple cider vinegar, garlic, and turmeric, Sakred Fire actively supports your digestive health while delivering a flavour experience you'll come back to again and again.
